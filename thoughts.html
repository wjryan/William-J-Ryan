I wrote this the night before some test I was stressing about and just couldnt think. It was raining and such.

		ABYSS 


		oh woe
		my mind is blank
		my eyes are blank
		my ears hear the drip drop
		of rain outside
		It came
		my center is calm
		Thoughts
		like large ocean farers,
		lightless dwellers,
		brush up against me
		leaving nothing
		but vague shape

		On the edges
		the slish slosh
		of an anxious shore

		15 March 2020
